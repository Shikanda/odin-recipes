Description

Beef stew is a hearty meal for all, this is the best beef stew recipe you can ever make with a Kenyan twist.

Ingredients

  • 500g beef.
  • Vegetable oil of your choice.
  • 2 tbsp of freshly crushed garlic cloves.
  • 1 cup red onion.
  • 1 cup crushed / diced tomatoes.
  • 1 cup water.
  • 1 cup of water.
  • Salt and Pepper to taste.
  • 3 tbsp fresh daina/coriander finely chopped.

Steps

  1. Heat a large pan on medium heat.
  2. Add the vegetable oil and onions then cook until soft.
  3. Add the garlic and spices, stir constantly to prevent any sticking.
  4. Add the beef and cook until fragrant then add toamtoes and the water.
  5. Bring to a slight boil, turn down the heat and simmer for 30 minutes.
  6. Remove from the heat and set aside until ready to serve.
